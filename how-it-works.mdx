MarketHive operates as a two-sided marketplace: employers supply free job listings, and job seekers pay a one-time fee to access the direct contact details hidden inside each listing. Neither side needs the other to invest upfront — employers risk nothing by posting, and job seekers only spend money on roles they've already evaluated and decided to pursue. The result is a faster, more intentional hiring process for everyone involved.

## How the Marketplace Works

On one side, employers create an account and post job listings at no cost. Each listing includes a job title, company name, location, salary range, and a full job description — all publicly visible and searchable. On the other side, job seekers browse those listings, use filters to narrow their search, and pay a small one-time fee when they find a role they want to act on. That payment instantly unlocks the employer's contact details so the job seeker can reach out directly.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Job Seekers">
    Follow these steps to go from discovering a listing to holding the employer's direct contact details in your inbox.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Browse Listings">
        Visit MarketHive and use the search bar or filters to explore available jobs. Filter by category, location, salary range, or job type to narrow results to roles that match your goals. All listing details — title, company, location, salary, and description — are visible to you at no charge.

        When you're ready to apply, click **Unlock Contact Details** on the listing. You'll be directed to the secure payment page powered by Minisend. Complete your one-time payment — there are no subscriptions or repeat charges for this listing.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Receive the Employer's Contact Details">
        As soon as your payment is confirmed, the employer's contact information is revealed instantly on-screen. At the same time, those details are sent directly to your registered email address so you always have them on hand.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Apply Directly">
        Use the unlocked phone number, email address, WhatsApp handle, or application link to contact the employer on your own terms — no middlemen, no automated rejection systems, just a direct conversation.
      </Step>

## Payment and Contact Reveal

MarketHive processes all payments securely through **Minisend**. When a job seeker clicks to unlock a listing, they're taken to the Minisend payment page where the transaction is completed safely and privately. The moment payment is confirmed, two things happen simultaneously:

1. **On-screen reveal** — The employer's contact details appear immediately on the listing page so you can act right away.
2. **Email delivery** — The same contact details are sent to the email address registered to your MarketHive account, giving you a permanent record.

Your full payment history and all previously unlocked contact details are always accessible from your account dashboard.
