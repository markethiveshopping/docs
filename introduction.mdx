MarketHive is a job lead marketplace built on a simple idea: the best hiring happens when talent and employers connect directly, without gatekeepers slowing things down. Employers post job listings at no cost, and job seekers browse those listings freely — paying only a one-time fee when they're ready to unlock the employer's direct contact information. No monthly subscriptions, no application queues, no third-party recruiters taking a cut. Just a clean, fast connection between the person hiring and the person who wants the job.

## Why MarketHive Is Different

Traditional job boards charge employers to post, force job seekers through opaque application systems, and rarely guarantee that a real human ever sees your résumé. MarketHive flips that model entirely. Listings are free to post and free to browse — you only pay when you find something worth pursuing. That one-time payment unlocks the employer's phone number, email, WhatsApp handle, or direct application link, putting the conversation entirely in your hands.

Every listing on MarketHive surfaces the job title, company name, location, salary range, and full job description before any payment is required. You make an informed decision first, then unlock the contact details that let you act on it.

## Benefits for Job Seekers

MarketHive is designed to give you control over your job search from the first click.

- **Pay once, not forever.** Each unlock is a single, one-time payment — there are no recurring charges or hidden fees.
- **Get direct contact details instantly.** The moment your payment processes, the employer's contact information appears on-screen and is sent to your email address.
- **Search on your terms.** Filter listings by category, location, salary, and job type so you only spend time on roles that match what you're actually looking for.
- **Save jobs for later.** Bookmark listings to revisit, compare, and unlock when you're ready.
- **Track your history.** Your account stores a full payment and unlock history so you never lose access to contact details you've already paid for.

## Benefits for Employers

Reaching the right candidates has never been more affordable — or more direct.

- **Post for free, always.** There is no charge to create a listing, update it, or keep it live. Zero cost, zero commitment.
- **Attract motivated applicants.** Job seekers who pay to unlock your contact details are actively interested in your role, not just casually scrolling.
- **Get found fast.** Featured listing options put your role at the top of search results, giving you even greater visibility among serious candidates.
- **Receive direct outreach.** Candidates contact you through the phone number, email, or WhatsApp you provide — no intermediary system to manage.
- **No subscription required.** Post as many roles as you need without worrying about tier limits or billing cycles.
