Your MarketHive profile is the central place to manage who you are on the platform. Keeping your details accurate — especially your email address — ensures you receive unlocked contact information without interruption and that your account stays secure. Whether you need to update your name, swap out a profile picture, or change your password, everything lives in one place.

## Accessing Your Profile Settings

To open your profile, click your avatar or name in the **top-right corner** of any page and select **Profile** from the dropdown menu. You'll land on your account settings page where all editable fields are displayed.

## Updating Your Profile Information

You can update the following fields at any time from the Profile Settings page:

- **Full name** — the name displayed on your account and in correspondence
- **Email address** — used for login, unlock confirmations, and all platform communications
- **Password** — your account login credential
- **Profile picture** — upload a photo or avatar to personalise your account

Edit any field and click **Save Changes** to apply your updates immediately.

<Warning>
Changing your email address affects where future unlock confirmation emails are delivered. Any contact details you purchase after the update will be sent to the new address. Double-check the new address before saving — a typo means you could miss important employer contact information. If you need to recover a misdirected email, contact [MarketHive support](mailto:support@markethive.com).
</Warning>

## Account Type: Job Seeker vs. Employer

MarketHive supports two account types, each with a distinct role on the platform:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Job Seeker" icon="briefcase" href="/account/profile#job-seeker">
    Browse and unlock employer contact details by paying a one-time fee per listing. Your payment history stores all unlocked contacts permanently.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Employer" icon="building" href="/account/profile#employer">
    Post job listings for free and receive notifications when job seekers unlock your listing's contact details.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Your account type is set when you register. If you need to operate in **both roles** — for example, you're a recruiter who also hires — you may need to maintain separate accounts for each role. Contact [MarketHive support](mailto:support@markethive.com) to discuss your situation before creating a second account.

## Changing Your Password

Keep your account secure by updating your password regularly. Use a strong, unique password that you don't use on other platforms.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Profile Settings">
    Click your avatar in the top-right corner and select **Profile** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Change Password'">
    Scroll to the **Security** section and click the **Change Password** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your current password">
    Type your existing password in the **Current Password** field to verify your identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter and confirm your new password">
    Type your new password in the **New Password** field, then retype it in the **Confirm New Password** field. Passwords must match before you can proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save Password**. You'll receive a confirmation email at your registered address. If you didn't make this change, contact support immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Use a password manager to generate and store a strong, unique password. Avoid reusing passwords from other sites.
</Tip>

## Deleting Your Account

If you'd like to permanently remove your MarketHive account, reach out to [MarketHive support](mailto:support@markethive.com) with your request. The support team will verify your identity and process the deletion.

Before requesting deletion, be aware of the following:

- All of your profile data, saved jobs, and notification preferences will be permanently erased.
- Your unlock history — including all previously accessed employer contact details — **cannot be recovered** after deletion.

<Note>
Deleting your account does not entitle you to refunds for any previous unlock payments. All purchases are final under MarketHive's payment policy. If you have a billing concern, raise it with support before requesting account deletion.
</Note>
