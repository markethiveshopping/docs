Your payment history is the permanent record of every contact unlock you've made on MarketHive. Every time you pay to reveal an employer's contact details, that transaction is logged here — along with the contact information itself — so you can return to it at any time without paying again. Think of it as your personal rolodex of every lead you've ever unlocked.

## Accessing Your Payment History

To view your payment history, click your avatar or name in the **top-right corner** of any page, then select **Payment History** from the account menu. Your full transaction log will load, sorted by most recent first.

## What Each Record Shows

Every entry in your payment history contains the following details:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Job & Company Info" icon="file-lines" href="/account/payment-history#record-details">
    The job title and company name (if the employer included one) associated with the listing you unlocked.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payment Details" icon="receipt" href="/account/payment-history#record-details">
    The date the payment was processed and the exact amount paid for that unlock.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact Information" icon="address-card" href="/account/payment-history#contact-info">
    The employer's revealed contact details — which may include a phone number, email address, WhatsApp contact, or direct application link.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transaction Receipt" icon="file-invoice" href="/account/payment-history#receipts">
    A downloadable receipt for each payment, useful for expense tracking or reimbursement.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Re-Accessing Contact Details You've Already Unlocked

Once you unlock a listing, the contact details are yours to revisit as many times as you like — **no second payment is ever required**. Simply open Payment History, locate the relevant job, and the contact details are displayed directly in the record.

<Tip>
Bookmark the Payment History page so you can quickly pull up employer contacts when you're ready to reach out — no searching required.
</Tip>

## Downloading or Copying Contact Details

To save contact details outside of MarketHive:

1. Open **Payment History** and locate the job record.
2. Click **View Details** on the relevant transaction.
3. Use the **Copy** button next to any contact field to copy it to your clipboard, or click **Download** to export the record as a PDF.

Keep downloaded records in a secure place, as they contain private employer contact information.

## A Note on Contact Detail Accuracy

<Note>
The contact details stored in your payment history reflect what the employer provided at the time you unlocked their listing. If an employer later updates their contact information on MarketHive, your history may display the refreshed details — or, in some cases, it may retain the original information depending on how the update was applied. If you suspect a contact detail is outdated, try reaching out through the listed channel and, if it fails, use the **Report an Issue** link on the record to flag it for review.
</Note>

## Payment Receipts

<Info>
Every successful unlock transaction automatically generates a payment receipt. You can access receipts directly from the Payment History page by clicking **View Receipt** on any transaction row. Receipts include the transaction ID, payment date, amount, and job listing reference — making them easy to use for personal expense records or employer reimbursements. Receipts are generated via Minisend, MarketHive's payment processor.
</Info>

## Filtering and Searching Your Payment History

If you have a long unlock history, use the search and filter tools at the top of the Payment History page to find specific records quickly:

<Accordion title="Filter by Date">
  Use the **Date Range** picker to narrow your history to a specific time window — for example, all unlocks made in the past 30 days or within a particular month. This is especially useful for reconciling expenses.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Search by Job Title">
  Type a keyword from the job title into the **Search** bar. MarketHive will surface all matching records instantly, so you can find a specific listing without scrolling through your entire history.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Filter by Company">
  If you remember the company name but not the job title, filter by **Company Name** to see all unlocks associated with that employer.
</Accordion>
