MarketHive is built to be straightforward — employers post jobs for free, and job seekers pay a small one-time fee to unlock the contact details they need to apply directly. Still have questions? Browse the sections below to find quick, clear answers to the most common things people ask about the platform.

## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is MarketHive?">
    MarketHive is a job lead marketplace that connects job seekers directly with employers. Employers post their job listings for free, and job seekers pay a one-time fee per listing to unlock the employer's contact details — such as a phone number, email address, WhatsApp contact, or direct application link. There are no subscriptions, no middlemen, and no lengthy application portals — just direct access to the person hiring.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is MarketHive free to use?">
    It depends on how you use the platform. **Employers** can post job listings completely free of charge, with no hidden costs or ongoing fees. **Job seekers** can browse and search all listings for free, but pay a one-time fee per listing to unlock the employer's contact details. You only pay for the listings you choose to unlock.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can use MarketHive?">
    MarketHive is open to anyone looking to hire or find work. If you are an employer — whether you run a small business, a startup, or a large organisation — you can post jobs and manage your listings through the employer dashboard. If you are a job seeker, you can create a free account, search and filter listings, save jobs you are interested in, and unlock contact details when you are ready to apply.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is MarketHive available on mobile?">
    Yes. MarketHive is designed mobile-first, so the platform works smoothly on smartphones and tablets without needing to download any app. Open your browser, visit the site, and access every feature — including searching listings, unlocking contact details, viewing your payment history, and managing your account — directly from your mobile device.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Job Seekers

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much does it cost to unlock a job's contact details?">
    The unlock fee varies by listing and is clearly displayed on each job post before you pay. Every fee is a one-time charge — you pay once per listing and gain permanent access to the contact details for that listing. There are no recurring charges, no subscriptions, and no extra fees once you have unlocked a listing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What contact information will I receive after paying?">
    After you complete your payment, the employer's contact details are revealed immediately on-screen and also sent to your registered email address. Depending on what the employer has provided, you may receive one or more of the following:

    - **Email address** — to send a direct application or enquiry
    - **Phone number** — to call or message the employer
    - **WhatsApp contact** — to reach the employer via WhatsApp
    - **Application link** — a direct URL to submit your application

    The contact details available for each listing are set by the employer when they post the job.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will I always receive the contact info by email?">
    Yes. Every time you successfully unlock a listing, the contact details are sent to the email address registered on your MarketHive account. The details are also displayed on-screen immediately after payment and are permanently accessible from your **Payment History** page in your account — so even if you miss the email, you can always retrieve your unlocked contact details by logging in.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I unlock the same job listing more than once?">
    No — and you will never need to. Once you unlock a listing, you have permanent access to the contact details. Log in to your account at any time and visit your **Payment History** page to view all the contact details you have previously unlocked. You will not be charged twice for the same listing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund if I change my mind after paying?">
    Because contact details are delivered instantly upon payment — both on-screen and by email — all unlock fees are **non-refundable** once the transaction is complete. We encourage you to read the full job listing carefully before choosing to unlock it. If you believe you were charged in error, contact our support team with your payment reference number and we will investigate promptly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if the contact details are incorrect or outdated?">
    Contact information is supplied directly by the employer and can occasionally become outdated if the employer changes their details after posting. If you find that the contact details you unlocked are inaccurate or no longer valid, use the **Report Listing** button on the job post to flag the issue. Our admin team reviews all flagged listings and will take appropriate action, which may include updating the listing or removing it from the platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Employers

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is posting a job really free?">
    Yes, completely. Creating an account and posting job listings on MarketHive costs you nothing. You can post as many listings as you need, manage them through your employer dashboard, and edit or remove them at any time — all for free. MarketHive generates revenue from job seekers who choose to unlock your contact details, not from employers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do job seekers find my listing?">
    Your listing appears in MarketHive's searchable job board as soon as it is approved and set to active. Job seekers can discover it by browsing the platform or by using the search and filter tools to narrow results by job type, location, industry, or other criteria. To maximise your listing's visibility, fill in all available fields — including a clear job title, full description, location, and relevant tags. You can also opt for a **Featured Listing** to appear prominently at the top of relevant search results.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I post anonymously without showing my company name?">
    You can choose to omit or abbreviate certain employer details in your listing, but MarketHive requires all listings to include valid contact details so that job seekers receive genuine value when they unlock a listing. Listings that are intentionally misleading or that provide false employer information are subject to removal under our platform policies. If you have concerns about visibility, contact our support team to discuss your options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I know when someone has unlocked my listing?">
    Your **Employer Dashboard** shows activity on all your listings, including how many times each listing has been unlocked. You can use this data to gauge interest in your open roles and decide whether to renew, edit, or boost a listing with a featured placement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit my job listing after it's published?">
    Yes. Log in to your account, go to your **Employer Dashboard**, and select the listing you want to update. You can edit the job title, description, location, contact details, and other fields at any time. Note that significant edits — particularly changes to contact details — may trigger a brief re-review to ensure the listing continues to meet platform guidelines before the changes go live.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Payments & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How are payments processed?">
    All payments on MarketHive are processed securely through **Minisend**, our trusted payment provider. When you choose to unlock a listing, you are directed to the Minisend payment page at `merchant.minisend.xyz/pay/rufus` to complete your transaction. Once your payment is confirmed, you are returned to MarketHive and the contact details are revealed immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my payment information secure?">
    Yes. MarketHive does not store your card or payment details on its servers. All payment data is handled entirely by Minisend, which uses industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your information. MarketHive only receives a confirmation of successful payment — never your raw financial data.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
    Accepted payment methods are determined by Minisend and are displayed on the payment page at the time of checkout. Supported methods typically include major debit and credit cards. For the most up-to-date list of accepted payment methods, check the Minisend payment page when you initiate an unlock.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I paid but didn't receive the contact info — what do I do?">
    First, check your **spam or junk folder** — the confirmation email occasionally lands there. Next, log in to your MarketHive account and visit your **Payment History** page, where your unlocked contact details are always stored and accessible regardless of whether the email was delivered. If the payment shows as successful but the contact details are missing, contact our support team and include your payment reference number so we can investigate and resolve the issue quickly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
