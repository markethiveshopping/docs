## Get Started as a Job Seeker

You're just a few steps away from landing your next opportunity. Here's how to go from sign-up to direct employer contact in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Free Account">
    Head to MarketHive and click **Sign Up**. Enter your name, email address, and a secure password, then verify your email to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search and Browse Listings">
    Use the search bar and filters to narrow down jobs by category, location, salary range, or job type. Every listing shows the job title, company name, location, salary, and full description — all before you pay a cent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock Employer Contact Details">
    Found a role you love? Pay a one-time unlock fee to instantly reveal the employer's phone number, email address, WhatsApp, or direct application link. Your contact details are also sent straight to your email for safekeeping.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Directly">
    Reach out to the employer through the contact method that works best for you — no recruiter filters, no application black holes, just a direct line to the decision-maker.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Employers always post for free.** MarketHive never charges employers to list a job or receive applications. The one-time fee is paid only by job seekers who choose to unlock a specific listing's contact details — keeping the platform open and accessible for everyone hiring.
</Note>
