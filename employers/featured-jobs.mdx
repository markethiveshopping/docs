Featured listings give your job post priority placement across MarketHive, putting your role in front of more job seekers before they scroll through standard results. While every employer can post jobs for free, featuring a listing is a paid upgrade that dramatically increases the number of eyes on your role — and, in turn, the number of seekers who pay to unlock your contact details.

## Where Featured Jobs Appear

Featured listings receive premium placement in three key areas of the platform:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Homepage Featured Section" icon="house" href="#where-featured-jobs-appear">
    A dedicated "Featured Jobs" carousel at the top of the MarketHive homepage — the first thing every visitor sees when they land on the platform.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Category Page Top Slots" icon="layer-group" href="#where-featured-jobs-appear">
    Your listing is pinned to the top of its industry category page, above all standard listings in that category.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Search Results Priority" icon="magnifying-glass" href="#where-featured-jobs-appear">
    When job seekers search for roles matching your title, location, or category, featured listings appear above standard results.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Featured Badge" icon="star" href="#visual-difference-between-featured-and-standard-listings">
    Your listing card displays a prominent **Featured** badge, signalling to job seekers that it's a highlighted opportunity and encouraging more clicks.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Feature a Listing

You can feature any of your active listings directly from your employer dashboard in a few steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your employer dashboard">
    Sign in to your employer account and navigate to **My Listings** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the listing you want to feature">
    Find the active listing you'd like to promote and click on it to open the listing detail view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Feature This Job'">
    Select the **Feature This Job** button. This opens the feature options panel where you can choose how long you want the boost to run.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a duration">
    Select how long you want your listing to be featured:

    | Duration | Best for |
    |---|---|
    | **7 days** | Roles with an urgent hiring deadline or high candidate volume |
    | **14 days** | Standard hiring cycles where you want steady visibility |
    | **30 days** | Longer-term searches or hard-to-fill specialist roles |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the feature payment">
    Review the cost for your chosen duration and complete the payment. MarketHive accepts all major credit and debit cards. You'll receive an email confirmation once the payment is processed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your listing is promoted immediately">
    There's no waiting period. As soon as payment is confirmed, your listing moves to the featured slots — on the homepage, in its category, and at the top of relevant search results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Visual Difference Between Featured and Standard Listings

Featured and standard listings appear throughout MarketHive, but featured listings are clearly distinguishable to job seekers:

<Accordion title="Featured Listing Appearance">
  Featured listings display a prominent gold **Featured** badge in the top-left corner of the listing card. The card itself uses a highlighted border to make it visually distinct from the standard listings below it. This badge signals credibility and urgency to job seekers, resulting in higher click-through rates compared to standard listings.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Standard Listing Appearance">
  Standard listings appear in the main listing feed below the featured slots. They display the same core information — job title, location, salary, and description preview — but without a badge or highlighted styling. Standard listings are ranked by recency and relevance.
</Accordion>

## Featured Listing Duration and Renewal

Your listing's featured status runs for the duration you selected at the time of purchase. Here's what to expect:

- **Automatic expiry** — when your featured period ends, your listing automatically reverts to standard status. It remains active and visible; it simply moves back into the regular listing feed.
- **Renewal reminders** — MarketHive sends you an email reminder 48 hours before your featured period expires so you can decide whether to renew.
- **Renewing a feature** — to extend or restart featured placement, go to **My Listings**, open the listing, and click **Feature This Job** again to purchase a new duration. There is no discount penalty for renewing early.
- **Featured + paused listings** — if you pause a listing while it is featured, the featured period is also paused. When you resume the listing, your remaining featured time picks up from where it left off.

<Tip>
  **Combine featured placement with a compelling description for the best results.** Featured status gets your listing seen — but the description is what converts viewers into paying unlockers. Lead with the most attractive aspects of the role (salary, flexibility, growth potential, company culture), write clearly and concisely, and make the requirements easy to scan. The combination of top-of-page placement and a high-quality listing consistently delivers the most unlocks per listing.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Featuring a listing is a **paid upgrade** — but remember, the base listing itself is always free. You only pay for featuring if you want priority placement. Your listing will continue to attract job seekers organically through standard search results and category pages at no cost.
</Note>
