Posting a job on MarketHive is completely free. There are no subscriptions, no per-post fees, and no hidden charges for employers. You provide your job details and contact information, and MarketHive keeps your contact details private until a job seeker pays a one-time fee to unlock them — meaning every person who reaches out to you has already invested in finding your role.

## How to Post a Job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to your employer account">
    Head to [markethive.com](https://markethive.com) and sign in with your employer credentials. If you don't have an account yet, select **Create an Employer Account** and complete the quick registration — it takes less than two minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Post a Job' from your dashboard">
    Once you're signed in, you'll land on your employer dashboard. Select the **Post a Job** button in the top-right corner to open the listing creation form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your job details">
    Complete all required fields that describe the role. This information is publicly visible to job seekers browsing MarketHive:

    - **Job title** — the exact role name (e.g., "Senior React Developer" or "Registered Nurse – ICU")
    - **Category** — choose the closest industry category (e.g., Technology, Healthcare, Finance, Retail, Education)
    - **Location** — enter a city, region, or select **Remote** if the role has no fixed location
    - **Salary** — enter a fixed amount or a range; you can also choose **Negotiable** if preferred
    - **Job type** — select Full-time, Part-time, Contract, or Remote
    - **Description** — write a detailed overview of the role, responsibilities, and requirements
    - **Company name** *(optional)* — display your organisation's name, or leave it blank for a confidential listing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your contact information">
    Enter the contact details that serious applicants will receive after paying the unlock fee. You can provide any combination of the following:

    - **Phone number** — direct line or WhatsApp-enabled number
    - **Email address** — a dedicated hiring inbox or your work email
    - **WhatsApp** — a WhatsApp number applicants can message directly
    - **Application link** — a URL to your careers page or application form

    At least one contact method is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and publish your listing">
    Preview how your listing will appear to job seekers. Check that the job details are accurate, the description is clear, and your contact information is correct. When you're satisfied, click **Publish Listing** — your job goes live immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fields Explained

Understanding each field helps you create a listing that attracts the right candidates and performs well in search results.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Job Title" icon="briefcase" href="#fields-explained">
    Use the exact job title a candidate would search for. Avoid internal codes or vague labels like "Opportunity Available" — clarity wins more clicks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Category" icon="tags" href="#fields-explained">
    Selecting the right category places your listing in the correct browse section. If no category is a perfect fit, choose the closest match.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Location" icon="location-dot" href="#fields-explained">
    City or region entries help location-based searches surface your listing. Select **Remote** if the role can be performed from anywhere.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Salary" icon="money-bill" href="#fields-explained">
    Listings with a salary range consistently attract more applicants. Even a broad range is more effective than leaving this field blank.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Job Type" icon="clock" href="#fields-explained">
    Specify whether the role is full-time, part-time, contract, or remote. Job seekers frequently filter by job type when browsing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Description" icon="file-lines" href="#fields-explained">
    Your description is the main selling point of your listing. Cover the role overview, key responsibilities, required qualifications, and any standout benefits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Company Name (optional)" icon="building" href="#fields-explained">
    Display your company name to build trust and brand recognition, or leave it blank to post a confidential listing — ideal for sensitive or replacement hires.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact Details" icon="address-card" href="#fields-explained">
    Phone, email, WhatsApp, or an application link — these are hidden from job seekers until they pay the unlock fee, ensuring you only hear from serious candidates.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Your **company name is optional**. If you're hiring confidentially — for example, replacing an existing employee or filling a sensitive position — simply leave the company name field blank. Your listing will display as "Confidential Employer."
</Note>

<Tip>
  **Write a description that sells the role, not just lists requirements.** Lead with what makes this job exciting — the team, the mission, the growth opportunities, or the flexibility. Candidates are more likely to unlock your contact details when the description answers "why should I work here?" alongside "what will I be doing?"
</Tip>

<Warning>
  **Always enter accurate, working contact details.** Job seekers pay a one-time fee specifically to receive this information. If your phone number is wrong, your email bounces, or your application link is broken, candidates will have paid for nothing — which damages your reputation on the platform and may result in your listing being reported. Double-check every contact field before you publish.
</Warning>
