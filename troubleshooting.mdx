Most issues on MarketHive can be resolved quickly without contacting support. This guide walks you through the most common problems job seekers and employers encounter — from missing unlock emails and failed payments to login difficulties and listing visibility — with clear, step-by-step fixes for each one.

---

## I paid but didn't receive the contact info email

Your unlocked contact details are always stored safely in your account, so even if the email did not arrive you have not lost access. Work through the steps below to locate your contact info and resolve the delivery issue.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your spam or junk folder">
    MarketHive unlock emails occasionally get filtered by email providers. Open your spam, junk, or promotions folder and search for an email from MarketHive. If you find it there, mark it as "Not Spam" to ensure future emails land in your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your registered email address">
    Log in to your MarketHive account and navigate to **Account Settings**. Verify that the email address on file is correct and that it is an inbox you have access to. If the address is wrong, update it and request a resend of the contact details from the relevant listing in your Payment History.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your contact info from Payment History">
    Your unlocked contact details are permanently stored in your account regardless of whether the email was delivered. Go to **Payment History** in your account menu, find the relevant listing, and click to view the full contact details. You can retrieve them here at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support if the issue persists">
    If your payment is showing as successful in Payment History but the contact details are missing or blank, reach out to our support team. Include your **account email address** and your **payment reference number** (found in Payment History) so we can locate your transaction and resolve the issue promptly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your unlocked contact details are always accessible from the **Payment History** page in your account — you never lose access just because an email was not delivered.
</Note>

## My payment failed or was declined

A failed payment means you were not charged. MarketHive only records a transaction and delivers contact details when a payment is fully confirmed — so if your payment did not go through, your account balance has not been affected.

Common reasons a payment may fail include:

- **Insufficient funds** on your card or account
- **Card or network error** caused by a temporary issue with your bank or internet connection
- **Session timeout** if you took too long to complete the payment page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Return to the job listing">
    Navigate back to the job listing you were trying to unlock. The listing will still show the **Unlock Contact Details** button because no successful payment was recorded.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate the payment again">
    Click **Unlock Contact Details** to start a fresh payment session. This generates a new, clean transaction — it is not a retry of the failed one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different payment method">
    On the Minisend payment page, select a different card or payment method if the one you used previously was declined. Ensure your card has sufficient funds and that online transactions are enabled by your bank.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You are **not charged** if your payment fails. A charge only appears when Minisend confirms a successful transaction and MarketHive delivers the contact details. If you see an unexpected charge despite no contact details being delivered, contact support immediately with your payment reference number.
</Note>

## I can't log in to my account

If you cannot access your MarketHive account, work through the checks below before contacting support.

Start by confirming you are entering the correct email address — the one you used to register — and double-check for typos. Passwords are case-sensitive, so make sure Caps Lock is off.

If your credentials look correct but login still fails, reset your password using the steps below.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Forgot Password' on the login page">
    On the MarketHive login screen, click the **Forgot Password?** link beneath the password field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your registered email address">
    Type in the email address associated with your account and submit the form. A password reset link will be sent to that address within a few minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox (and spam folder)">
    Open the reset email from MarketHive. If it is not in your inbox, check your spam or junk folder. Click the reset link inside the email — note that reset links expire after a short period, so use it promptly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a new password">
    Follow the link to create a new password. Once saved, return to the login page and sign in with your new credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If resetting your password does not restore access, your account may have been suspended or deleted due to a policy violation or inactivity. Contact our support team with your account email address and we will investigate and advise on next steps.

## A job listing seems outdated or the contact info is wrong

Contact information on MarketHive is provided directly by employers and reflects what they supplied at the time of posting. Employers occasionally change their phone numbers, email addresses, or hiring processes after a listing goes live, which can result in contact details that are no longer accurate.

If you find that the contact details you unlocked are incorrect, invalid, or no longer active:

1. Use the **Report Listing** button on the job post page to flag the listing for review.
2. Briefly describe the issue in the report form — for example, "Phone number is not in service" or "Email address bounces."
3. Submit the report. MarketHive's admin team reviews all flagged listings and will contact the employer to request updated information, or remove the listing if it cannot be resolved.

<Info>
  Reporting a listing helps protect other job seekers on the platform. Our admin team takes all reports seriously and aims to review flagged content promptly.
</Info>

## I posted a job and it's not appearing in search

New listings and recently edited listings go through a short review process before they appear publicly in search results. This is normal and usually resolves within a few minutes.

If your listing has not appeared after a reasonable wait, check the following:

1. **Log in to your Employer Dashboard** and locate the listing in question.
2. **Check the listing status.** Each listing displays one of the following statuses:
   - **Active** — the listing is live and visible in search results.
   - **Under Review** — the listing is being checked for compliance with platform guidelines and will go live once approved.
   - **Rejected** — the listing did not meet platform policies. You will receive guidance on what needs to change before resubmitting.
3. **If the status is Under Review**, allow additional time for the admin team to complete their check. You do not need to resubmit.

<Tip>
  Listings with complete, detailed information — including a clear job title, full job description, location, contact details, and relevant category tags — pass the review process faster. Incomplete listings are more likely to be flagged for manual review or returned for more information.
</Tip>
