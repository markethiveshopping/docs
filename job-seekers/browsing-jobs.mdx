MarketHive's job board gives you instant access to thousands of live listings posted by employers across every industry. Whether you know exactly what you're looking for or you're exploring your options, the search and filter tools help you cut through the noise and surface the opportunities that matter most to you — all before spending a single penny.

## Using the Search Bar

Type any keyword into the search bar at the top of the job board to find relevant listings instantly. You can search by job title, skill, company name, or any term that appears in the listing description.

**Tips for better search results:**

- Use specific job titles (e.g., "React Developer" instead of "Developer") for tighter results
- Search by skill or tool (e.g., "Python", "QuickBooks", "Figma") to find roles that match your expertise
- Try broader terms like "marketing" or "finance" when you want to explore a wider set of opportunities

## Filtering Listings

Narrow your results using the filter panel to focus only on jobs that match your criteria. The following filters are available:

You can combine multiple filters at once. Your active filters are displayed as tags above the results — click the **×** on any tag to remove it individually, or select **Clear All** to reset.

## What You See on Each Listing Card

Every job card on the board shows you enough information to judge whether the role is worth pursuing:

- **Job title** — the position being advertised
- **Company name** — shown when the employer chooses to display it (some post anonymously)
- **Location** — city, region, remote status, or a combination
- **Salary** — the listed rate or range (hourly, monthly, or annual)
- **Description preview** — a short excerpt from the full job description

Click any card to open the full listing and read the complete job description.

## What Stays Hidden Until You Pay

To protect employer privacy and ensure the quality of leads, certain details are locked behind a one-time unlock payment. You will **not** see the following until you complete payment:

- Employer phone number
- Employer email address
- WhatsApp contact link
- Application link or instructions

Once you unlock a listing, these details are revealed instantly on-screen and sent directly to your email. See [Unlock Employer Contact Details](/job-seekers/unlocking-contacts) for the full process.

<Tip>
  Not ready to unlock a listing right now? Hit the bookmark icon on any job card to save it to your shortlist. You can come back and compare multiple listings before deciding which contacts to unlock. Learn more in [Saved Jobs](/job-seekers/saved-jobs).
</Tip>

## Featured Jobs

At the top of the job board you'll find a **Featured** section highlighting a curated selection of listings. Featured jobs are pinned above the general results and are refreshed regularly. They represent active, high-quality postings — a great place to start if you're not sure where to begin.

Featured listings follow the same unlock model as all other jobs: the preview is free to browse, and contact details are revealed after a one-time payment.
