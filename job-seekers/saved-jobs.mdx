Not every great listing demands an immediate decision. The Saved Jobs feature lets you bookmark listings as you browse so you can build a personal shortlist, revisit them at your own pace, and compare multiple opportunities side by side — all before spending anything. It's your own private list of roles worth keeping an eye on.

## How to Save a Job

Saving a listing takes a single click. On any job card in the search results or on the full listing page, look for the **bookmark icon** in the top-right corner of the card and click it.

- A filled bookmark icon (🔖) means the job is saved to your list
- An unfilled bookmark icon means the job has not been saved yet
- You can save a job at any point — while browsing search results or while reading a full listing

You must be signed in to your MarketHive account to save jobs. If you're not signed in, clicking the bookmark icon will prompt you to log in or create a free account.

## Accessing Your Saved Jobs

Your full saved list is available from your account dashboard at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your account dashboard">
    Click your profile picture or name in the top navigation bar to open the account menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'Saved Jobs'">
    Choose **Saved Jobs** from the menu. This takes you to your complete shortlist of bookmarked listings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse and act on your saved listings">
    From your saved list, you can read any listing in full, unlock contact details, or remove listings you're no longer interested in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Saved List

Keep your shortlist clean and useful with these management options:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Remove a Saved Job" icon="bookmark-slash" href="/job-seekers/saved-jobs#managing">
    Click the filled bookmark icon on any saved listing — in your list or on the listing page — to remove it from your shortlist instantly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sort by Date Saved" icon="arrow-down-wide-short" href="/job-seekers/saved-jobs#managing">
    Your saved jobs list is ordered by the date you bookmarked each listing, with the most recently saved appearing first.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

There is no limit to how many jobs you can save. Build as large a shortlist as you like and trim it down as you narrow your options.

<Note>
  Saving a job is completely free. You only pay when you choose to unlock the employer's contact details for a specific listing. Browsing, saving, and comparing jobs never costs anything.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Use your saved list as a comparison tool before committing to any unlock payments. Save several listings that interest you, then review them together to weigh up salary, location, job type, and description quality. Unlock the contacts for your top picks first — you can always come back for others later.
</Tip>

## If a Listing Is Removed by the Employer

Employers can remove or deactivate their listings at any time. If a listing you've saved is taken down:

- The listing will be marked as **No Longer Available** in your saved list rather than disappearing silently
- You will not be charged anything — saving a job creates no payment obligation
- The **Unlock Contact Details** button will be disabled for deactivated listings

<Info>
  If you've already unlocked a listing's contact details before it was removed, those details remain accessible in your [Payment History](/account/payment-history). Deactivating a listing does not delete information you've already paid for.
</Info>
