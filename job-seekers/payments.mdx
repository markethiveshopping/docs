MarketHive keeps its pricing model simple and transparent: you only ever pay to unlock the contact details of a specific job listing you want to pursue. There are no subscriptions, no recurring charges, and no cost to browse. Every payment is a standalone, one-time transaction tied to a single listing — you stay in full control of what you spend.

## How Pricing Works

Each job listing on MarketHive has a fixed one-time unlock fee displayed on the listing page. Paying this fee reveals the employer's contact details for that listing only.

Key things to understand about pricing:

- **No subscription required** — you are never enrolled in any recurring plan
- **Pay per listing** — each unlock is an independent transaction
- **Fee shown upfront** — the exact amount is displayed before you commit to payment
- **Instant access** — contact details are revealed the moment your payment clears

## Payment Processing via Minisend

All payments on MarketHive are processed securely through **Minisend**, a trusted payment gateway. When you click **Unlock Contact Details**, you are taken to the Minisend-hosted payment page where your transaction is completed safely.

MarketHive never stores your card details or financial information — everything is handled end-to-end by Minisend's secure infrastructure.

### Accepted Payment Methods

Minisend supports a range of payment options, including:

- **Debit and credit cards** (Visa, Mastercard)
- **Mobile money** (where available in your region)
- Additional methods as supported by Minisend in your country

The available options for your location will be displayed automatically on the Minisend payment page.

## The Payment Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Unlock Contact Details' on a listing">
    Open the job listing you want to apply for and click the **Unlock Contact Details** button. Review the unlock fee shown on the page before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're taken to the secure Minisend payment page">
    Your browser navigates to the Minisend payment page at `merchant.minisend.xyz/pay/rufus`. Here you'll see the payment amount, available methods, and a secure form to enter your details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the payment">
    Enter your payment information and confirm the transaction. Minisend processes the payment securely in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're redirected back to MarketHive with contact info revealed">
    Once payment is confirmed, Minisend returns you to the MarketHive listing page. The employer's contact details are immediately unlocked and displayed in full.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation email sent with contact details">
    A confirmation email is sent instantly to your registered email address. It contains the full contact information so you have a permanent record for your own reference.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Always use a real, active email address on your MarketHive account. Your unlocked contact details are sent to this address immediately after payment — if it's incorrect or inaccessible, you may not be able to retrieve them. You can update your email in **Account Settings** before making a payment.
</Warning>

## Viewing Your Payment History

Every successful unlock payment is recorded in your account. To access your payment history:

1. Sign in to your MarketHive account
2. Navigate to [Account → Payment History](/account/payment-history)
3. Find the transaction you need — each entry shows the job title, date, amount paid, and a link to view the unlocked contact details again

Your payment history is permanent. You can return to it at any time to retrieve contact information from listings you've previously unlocked.

## Refund Policy

All unlock payments on MarketHive are **non-refundable**.

Because contact details are revealed to you instantly at the moment of payment, the service is considered fully delivered as soon as your transaction is confirmed. We are unable to reverse or refund payments once the information has been disclosed.

<Info>
  Before unlocking any listing, read the full job description carefully and confirm it's the right opportunity for you. If you have any concerns about a specific listing — such as suspected inaccurate information — contact [MarketHive Support](/troubleshooting) before completing payment.
</Info>
