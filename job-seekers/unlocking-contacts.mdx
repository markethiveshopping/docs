MarketHive uses a simple unlock model to connect job seekers directly with employers. Browsing every listing on the board is completely free — you only pay when you find a role you genuinely want to pursue. A single one-time payment reveals the employer's contact details instantly, giving you everything you need to reach out and apply without any ongoing subscription or hidden fees.

## How to Unlock a Listing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a job you want to apply for">
    Search and filter the job board until you find a listing that matches your skills and goals. Read the full description to make sure it's the right fit before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Unlock Contact Details'">
    On the listing page, click the **Unlock Contact Details** button. This confirms your intent to purchase the contact information for that specific listing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the secure payment via Minisend">
    You'll be taken to the Minisend secure payment page to complete the transaction. Enter your payment details and confirm. Your payment is processed safely — MarketHive never stores your card information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact details are revealed instantly on-screen">
    As soon as payment is confirmed, you're redirected back to the listing and the employer's contact information is displayed in full. No waiting, no manual review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="A copy is sent to your registered email address">
    A confirmation email is sent immediately to the email address on your MarketHive account. It contains the full contact details so you have a permanent record even after leaving the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Contact Information Gets Revealed

The specific details you receive depend on what the employer chose to provide when posting the listing. Any combination of the following may be unlocked:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Phone Number" icon="phone" href="/job-seekers/unlocking-contacts#contact-types">
    A direct phone number to call or text the employer or hiring manager.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Address" icon="envelope" href="/job-seekers/unlocking-contacts#contact-types">
    The employer's preferred email address for receiving applications or enquiries.
  </Card>
  <Card title="WhatsApp" icon="whatsapp" href="/job-seekers/unlocking-contacts#contact-types">
    A WhatsApp contact link so you can message the employer directly from your phone.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Application Link" icon="arrow-up-right-from-square" href="/job-seekers/unlocking-contacts#contact-types">
    A direct URL to the employer's application form or careers page.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Every listing clearly shows how many contact methods are available before you pay, so you know what you're unlocking.

<Note>
  Each job listing is unlocked separately with its own one-time payment. Unlocking one listing does not grant access to contact details on any other listing. There are no bundles or subscription tiers — you pay only for the specific listings you want to pursue.
</Note>

<Info>
  Every listing you unlock is permanently saved to your payment history and remains accessible at any time from your account dashboard. You will never lose access to contact details you've already paid for — simply revisit your payment history to retrieve them.
</Info>
