Getting started on MarketHive takes just a few minutes. Whether you're a job seeker ready to browse listings or an employer looking to post your first role for free, the sign-up process is the same — you simply choose your account type along the way, and MarketHive tailors your dashboard and features accordingly. Create your account once, and everything you unlock, save, or post stays tied to it permanently.

## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to MarketHive and Click Sign Up">
    Open MarketHive in your browser and click the **Sign Up** button in the top-right corner of the homepage. This takes you to the registration form where you'll enter your basic details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Name, Email, and Password">
    Fill in your full name, a valid email address, and a strong password. Use an email address you check regularly — MarketHive sends unlocked employer contact details to this address after each payment, so keeping it current is important.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Account Type">
    Select whether you're joining as a **Job Seeker** or an **Employer**. This determines what features appear in your dashboard. Don't worry if your needs change — you can update your account type from your profile settings later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email Address">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from MarketHive. Click the confirmation link inside to activate your account. If you don't see the email within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder and request a new one from the login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    Once your email is verified, you're prompted to complete your profile. Add a profile photo, a short bio, and any other details relevant to your account type. A complete profile builds trust with employers and helps job seekers understand your company when browsing listings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Keep your email address up to date in your account settings. Every time you unlock a job listing, MarketHive sends the employer's contact details — phone number, email, WhatsApp, or application link — directly to your registered email. If your email is outdated, you may miss that delivery and would need to return to your payment history in the dashboard to retrieve the contact details.
</Tip>

## Log In and Reset Your Password

Once your account is active, log in anytime by clicking **Log In** on the MarketHive homepage and entering your email address and password.

**Forgot your password?** Follow these steps to regain access:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Forgot Password'">
    On the login page, click the **Forgot Password?** link below the password field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Registered Email">
    Type in the email address associated with your MarketHive account and click **Send Reset Link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Inbox">
    Open the password reset email from MarketHive. Click the reset link inside — note that the link expires after 30 minutes for security reasons.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a New Password">
    Enter and confirm your new password. Once saved, you're logged in automatically and can continue where you left off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If you no longer have access to the email address on your account, contact MarketHive support directly to verify your identity and recover your account. Do not create a duplicate account — your payment history and saved jobs are tied to your original account.
</Warning>
